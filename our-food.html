Is your pup getting in all the essential nutrients for optimal health?

Feeding fresh-frozen or home-cooked foods may cause dogs to miss out on the necessary mix of nutrients that they need on a daily basis. Each serving of our kibble is made with U.S-sourced quality ingredients that is blended to create the perfect mix of proteins, carbohydrates, fats and vitamins to form a complete and balanced meal for your dog.  

Our Food

Formulated by veterinarians and pet nutritionists, we use the highest quality ingredients to make our formulas here in the USA!

Our Formulas
We start off with one of our base-proteins which include our Chef’s Chicken, Tender Turkey, Lavish Lamb, Tasty Trout or Savory Salmon. We then complete the meal by adding the necessary nutrients to meet your pup’s unique nutritional needs.
(Did you know that we supplement all of our formulas with Taurine to help those that are pre-disposed to potential heart issues!)
Superior Ingredients

Your Dog Will Love It!

Our superior ingredients translate to great palatability – even the pickiest of the picky and the finickiest of the finicky will love it!

Maximum Nutrition

We cook our kibble at a lower temperature than most in order to maximize nutrition so that our vitamins and minerals don’t lose their nutritional value throughout the cooking process.

No Fillers

We only use low-ash, high-grade proteins that don't produce the “filler” content often seen in proteins of lesser quality.

All of our formulas meet AAFCO (the pet food authority that is made up of all the individual US States and the US FDA) standards.

Crafted Kibble’s Core 4 Support
Our recipes focus on easy digestion, and our Core 4 System works to ensure that your pet stays happy and healthy. Core 4 System supports great gut health and is designed for necessary absorption of important nutrients. Our kibble works from the inside out to provide everything your pup needs from its food.

Our Standards
All our kibble is made in the USA under strict guidelines and quality control procedures. The plants are GSFI certified from BRC (Global Food Safety Initiative) – one of the highest quality control and assurance standards in the world – and exceed GMP (Good Manufacturing Practices) required by the governing bodies and AAFCO guidelines.

Although it makes it a little costlier, this ensures the greatest quality control processes and transparency, which gives both us and our pet-parents the peace-of-mind we all deserve.
